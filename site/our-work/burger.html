Superior Builders & Remodelers is a second generation company that has been building and remodeling East Bay homes since 1947. We are a small, efficient team of tight-knit professionals that are well known in our community. We take pride in doing quality work and giving the best possible service to our customers.

David Lorber has been with the business for 35 years, and his associate, Jerry Johnson, has worked alongside him for 30 years. Superior's employees, whose average tenure with the company is in excess of 25 years, are all dedicated, professional craftsmen.
